## Project Overview

This project analyzes supply chain operations using Power BI. The dashboard provides insights into revenue, product sales, shipping performance, inventory levels, manufacturing lead time, supplier performance, and product quality.

The goal is to identify operational inefficiencies and support better business decisions.

## Objectives

- Analyze overall revenue and product sales
- Monitor supplier performance
- Track manufacturing and supplier lead times
- Evaluate shipping costs
- Identify defect rates
- Improve supply chain efficiency


## Tools Used

- Power BI
- SQL
- Microsoft Excel
- DAX

## Dataset

The dataset contains:

- Product Type
- SKU
- Price
- Revenue Generated
- Number of Products Sold
- Stock Levels
- Shipping Costs
- Shipping Carriers
- Transportation Modes
- Manufacturing Lead Time
- Supplier Lead Time
- Defect Rates
- Supplier Name
- Inspection Results


## Dashboard KPIs

- Total Revenue
- Total Products Sold
- Average Shipping Cost
- Average Manufacturing Lead Time
- Average Supplier Lead Time
- Average Defect Rate

## Business Insights

- Products with the highest revenue
- Supplier performance comparison
- Transportation cost analysis
- Manufacturing efficiency
- Quality inspection trends
- Inventory optimization opportunities
